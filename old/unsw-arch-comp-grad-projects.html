UNSW's Arch comp course students need to find a 'real' client for their final project. I mentioned before that I had one student, but now I'm lucky enough to be supervising three this semester!

They are chronicling their work here, but the general gist of things is that Jen is doing AR, Mike is doing caves, and Jay is doing Kinnect UIs. I've explained their work in terms of the technology that they are using, which is exactly what I've asked them to do! I've asked them to frame their research in terms of an architectural question that they can try to answer by using a technological approach. I've also asked for some kind of empirical metric of efficacy for this approach vs the status quo.
